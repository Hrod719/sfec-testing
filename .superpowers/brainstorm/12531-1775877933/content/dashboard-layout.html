Key Design Decisions

Hero Header
Dark branded header contains nav, cycle selector, and all 6 stat cards. This IS the executive summary — everything above the fold tells the story.
Readiness %
Test Readiness percentage with progress bar — the single most important number. Calculated as students with complete data / total. Green when >90%.
Flagged Card
Red-tinted stat card for compliance issues draws the eye. Pairs with the alert banner below to create urgency without being obnoxious.
Light Content Zone
White cards on #F7F8FA — maximum readability for tables and detail views. Clean contrast with the branded header above.
